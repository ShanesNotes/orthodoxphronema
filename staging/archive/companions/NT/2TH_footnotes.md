### 3:13
*(anchor: 2TH.3:13)*

A warning filled with great wisdom. While sin is burdensome, we can also grow tired

and fatigued by doing good. Paul, as our spiritual director, cautions us not to cease or

tire of good works.


### 3:17
*(anchor: 2TH.3:17)*

Paul's authorship is emphasized here because of the fraudulent letters mentioned in

2:2.
