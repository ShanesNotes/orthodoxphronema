# Lectionary Notes: JOH

**7:37-52** — [[JOH.7:37]]–52; 8:12: This passage is read on Pentecost (Trinity Sunday), the seventh Sunday after Pascha (Easter).

**9:1-38** — This passage is read on the sixth Sunday of Pascha, called the Sunday of the Blind Man.

**10:9-16** — This passage is read on days commemorating a holy hierarch.

**11:1-45** — This passage is read on the Saturday before Palm Sunday, called Lazarus Saturday.

**12:1-18** — This passage is read on Palm Sunday, the Feast of the Entrance of Our Lord into Jerusalem.

**13:3-17** — This passage is read on Holy Thursday, the Thursday before Pascha (Easter).

**15:17-2** — This passage is read on days commemorating a holy martyr.

**17:1-13** — This passage is read on the Feast of the Fathers of the First Ecumenical Council.

**19:6-11** — [[JOH.19:6]]–11, 13–20, 25–28, 30–35: This passage is read on September 14, the Feast of the Exaltation of the Holy Cross.

**19:25-27** — (no occasion text)

**19:31-37** — This passage is read on Great and Holy Friday, the Friday before Pascha (Easter).

**20:19-31** — This passage is read on the second Sunday of Pascha, called Thomas Sunday.

**19:25-27; 21:24, 25** — This passage is read on September 26, the Feast of St. John the Theologian.
