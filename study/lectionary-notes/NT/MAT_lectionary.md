# Lectionary Notes: MAT

**9:36** — [[MAT.9:36]]; 10:1–8: This passage is read on June 30, the Synaxis of the Twelve Apostles.

**10:1** — [[MAT.10:1]], [[MAT.10:5]]–8: This passage is read on days commemorating holy unmercenary healers.

**10:16-22** — This passage is read on days commemorating holy martyrs and on August 1, the Feast of the Procession of the Cross.

**10:32** — (no occasion text)

**11:2-15** — (no occasion text)

**11:27-30** — This passage is read on days commemorating holy monks.

**15:21-28** — This passage is read on days commemorating female martyrs.

**16:13-19** — This passage is read on June 29, the Feast of Sts. Peter and Paul.

**17:1-9** — This passage is read on August 6, the Feast of the Holy Transfiguration of Our Lord Jesus Christ.

**18:10-20** — This passage is read on the Monday after Pentecost, called the Day of the Holy Spirit.

**23:29-39** — This passage is read on days commemorating holy prophets.

**24:3-35** — This passage is read on Holy Monday, the Monday before Pascha (Easter).

**24:36-2** — This passage is read on Holy Tuesday, the Tuesday before Pascha (Easter).

**25:1-13** — This passage is read on days commemorating holy nuns.

**25:31-46** — This passage is read on the second Sunday before Great Lent, called Meat Fare Sunday, or Sunday of the Last Judgment.

**26:1, 2** — (no occasion text)

**27:1-61** — This passage is read at Vespers of Great and Holy Friday, the Friday before Pascha (Easter).

**27:62-66** — This passage is read at Matins of Great and Holy Saturday, the Saturday before Pascha (Easter).

**28:1-20** — This passage is read on Great and Holy Saturday.
