# Lectionary Notes: DAN

**2:31-36** — Dan 2:31–36, 44–45: This passage is read in Church on Christmas Eve.

**3:1-90** — This passage is read on Great and Holy Saturday.

**1:1-25** — This passage is read on the Sunday before Christmas, called the Sunday of the Holy Fathers.

**2:1-12** — (no occasion text)

**2:13-23** — This passage is read on December 26, the Synaxis of the Theotokos.

**3:13-17** — (no occasion text)

**5:14-19** — This passage is read on days commemorating holy hierarchs.

**6:14-21** — This passage is read on the Sunday before Great Lent, called Cheese Fare Sunday or Forgiveness Sunday.

**7:12-21** — This passage is read on days commemorating holy monks.

**9:9-13** — This passage is read on November 16, the Feast of the Apostle Matthew.
