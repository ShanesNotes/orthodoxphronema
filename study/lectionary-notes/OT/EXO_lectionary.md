# Lectionary Notes: EXO

**33:11-23** — [[EXO.33:11]]–23; 34:4–6, 8: These verses are read as the second reading during the Feast of the Transfiguration.

**40:1-5** — [[EXO.40:1]]–5, 9, 10, 16, 34, 35: These verses are read during the Feast of the Entry of the Most Holy Mother of God into the Temple.
