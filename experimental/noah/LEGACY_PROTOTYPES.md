# Noah Legacy Prototypes

> Classification note for sandbox materials that are no longer the active experiment
> path.

## Status

The active experimental runtime path is documented in:

- `experimental/noah/MINIMAL_RUNTIME_RUNBOOK.md`

The files below are retained as legacy prototypes only:

- `experimental/noah/harness.py`
- `experimental/noah/manifest_generator.py`
- `experimental/noah/noah_manifest.json`
- `experimental/noah/noah_system_prompt.md`
- `experimental/noah/noah.service`
- `experimental/noah/noah.timer`

## Why They Are Legacy

- They center a chapter/day manifest rather than the official Noah pericope queue.
- They embed concrete machine, service, or runner assumptions.
- They predate the current queue/bundle packet contract and local `bible/` scripture
  mirror model.

## Retention Rule

Keep these files for historical context, comparison, or future adaptation, but do not
treat them as the default implementation target.
