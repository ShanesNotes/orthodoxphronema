# Index to Annotations

INDEX TO ANNOTATIONS
This is a thorough, but not exhaustive, index to topics and names discussed in the
annotations and articles of the Orthodox Study Bible. For further information on topics, see
the Glossary and the Index to Articles. Entries that cover several verses may refer to two or
more annotations within a passage of Scripture.
In addition to the usual book name abbreviations, the abbreviation “Dan-Sus” refers to the
story of Susanna found at the beginning of the Book of Daniel, which is not numbered as a
chapter. The abbreviation “Dan-Bel” refers to the story of Bel and the Serpent, found at the
end of the Book of Daniel and also not numbered as a chapter.
